# 1. Organization Philosophy
## 1.1 The Centaur Model
NoDrftSystems operates as a "Centaur Organization" where human expertise and AI capabilities combine to deliver superior outcomes. Every team member is equipped with AI tools and trained to leverage them effectively.
## 1.2 Core Principles
AI-Augmented Excellence: Every role is designed around human-AI collaboration. Team members are evaluated on their ability to leverage AI tools effectively.
Specialist Network: Core team maintains generalist capabilities while accessing deep expertise through a vetted specialist bench.
Continuous Learning: Investment in ongoing education and skill development is a core organizational priority.
Outcome-Focused: Performance measured by delivered value, not hours worked or activities completed.
## 1.3 Team Composition Strategy
The optimal team composition balances core capabilities with flexible specialist access. This approach maximizes agility while ensuring deep expertise is available when needed.

## 2.3 AI Agent Staff
NoDrftSystems deploys specialized AI agents as part of the team. These agents handle specific tasks under human supervision:

Design Assistant: Asset generation, layout variations, accessibility checks
QA Assistant: Test case generation, regression testing, bug triage

# 4. Specialist Bench
## 4.1 Specialist Categories
The specialist bench provides on-demand expertise for specific project needs. All specialists are pre-vetted and have established working relationships with NoDrftSystems.
## 4.2 Specialist Vetting Process
Portfolio review and reference checks
Technical assessment or test project
Cultural fit interview
Trial engagement (small paid project)
Ongoing performance evaluation
## 4.3 Specialist Management
Quarterly performance reviews for active specialists
Annual bench refresh and skill assessment
Rate negotiation and contract updates
Knowledge sharing and training opportunities
Referral program for new specialist recruitment
