# 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
## The Centaur Organization
NoDrftSystems operates as a Centaur Organization—a hybrid entity where human strategic oversight merges with AI agent execution under a three-tier supervisor architecture. Unlike traditional agencies that deploy AI as a peripheral tool, we embed AI agents as core staff members with defined roles, KPIs, tool stacks, and human oversight protocols governed by the Tier 1 Supervisor Layer.
## Structural Arbitrage

## Immediate Market Position
We launch with four validated portfolio assets—CasaClara (PropTech), RepairForge (Services AI), Peak Optimizer Pro (Real Estate Investment), and The Walcott Co & Press (Publishing)—eliminating the cold-start credibility gap. Our $15,000 seed capital funds 5+ months of runway to cash-flow positive when paired with rigorous client qualification protocols.

## The Non-Negotiables
AI Reliability Engineer (ARE) is the first hire—without this human validation layer, AI agents produce "drift" (technically functional code that fails business objectives).
Price in USD, Deliver in COP—maintain currency arbitrage; never invoice U.S. clients in pesos.
Close First Client in 60 Days—revenue validation trumps perfect systems.
Zero Drift Client Protocol—one misqualified client bankrupts a $15K seed-stage company; rigorous qualification is mandatory (see Addendum 5: Client Evaluation Scorecard).
Human Authority is Non-Delegable—AI agents produce, review, recommend, and prepare. They do not authorize. No agent signs, sends to clients, or deploys to production without documented human approval (see Addendum 18: Human Handoff Protocol).

# 2. VISION, MISSION & CORE VALUES
## Vision
To become the most trusted precision-engineering firm for digital systems in the Americas—where zero drift is not a slogan but a measurable standard enforced by the three-tier AI architecture with human governance.
## Mission
To design and develop modern digital ecosystems—websites, applications, and integrated software solutions—that improve operational efficiency, strengthen market presence, and achieve sustainable, compounding growth through AI-augmented delivery with human validation.
## Core Values
Zero Drift: Every output is intentional, accurate, and aligned with client objectives. No hallucination. No scope creep. Enforced by the QAS (QA Supervisor) agent.
Human + AI Synergy: The speed and scale of AI combined with the judgment and creativity of humans. Neither replaced; both amplified. Governed by the Tier 1 Supervisor Layer.
Transparent Value: Clients always understand what they are getting, why it costs what it costs, and how it drives ROI.
Security & IP Integrity: Enterprise-grade protection for data, code provenance, and intellectual property. Managed by SCA (Security/Compliance Agent) and IPGA (IP Guardian Agent).
Risk-Asymmetry Awareness: We price for uncertainty, qualify for fit, and maintain kill switches to protect margin and morale.

# 3. THE CENTAUR MODEL: HUMAN + AI STAFF ARCHITECTURE
## 3.1 Philosophy
The Centaur Model divides labor along cognitive lines:
AI Agents: Handle high-volume, pattern-based, and research-intensive tasks (data enrichment, code scaffolding, content generation, security scanning). Governed by the Master Orchestrator Agent (MOA).
Human Team: Handles strategic ambiguity, client relationships, ethical judgment, final quality assurance, and liability-bearing decisions.
Critical Protocol: Every AI agent output requires human validation before client delivery. The AI Reliability Engineer (ARE) maintains a 95%+ defect capture rate and enforces the Context-First Definition of Done.
