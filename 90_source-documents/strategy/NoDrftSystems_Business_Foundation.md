# 4. Service Catalog
## 4.1 Service Tiers Overview
## 4.2 Service Definitions
### Diagnostic: Product Audit & Roadmap
A comprehensive assessment of your current product, technical architecture, and market position. Deliverables include a detailed audit report, prioritized roadmap, and technical recommendations.
Deliverables:
Technical architecture review and risk assessment
UX/UI audit with specific improvement recommendations
Competitive analysis and positioning recommendations
90-day prioritized roadmap with effort estimates
AI integration opportunity assessment
### Foundation: MVP Build
End-to-end design and development of a minimum viable product ready for market validation. Includes strategy, design, development, testing, and deployment.
Includes:
Product strategy and feature prioritization
Complete UX/UI design (up to 20 screens)
Full-stack development (React/Node.js or equivalent)
AI integration (where applicable)
Comprehensive testing and QA
Deployment and launch support
30-day post-launch support
### Growth: Feature Expansion
Extension of an existing product with new features, integrations, or capabilities. Designed for products with product-market fit seeking to expand functionality.
Includes:
Feature specification and UX design
Full development and integration
Regression testing of existing functionality
Performance optimization
Documentation and knowledge transfer
### Scale: Platform Evolution
Major platform upgrades, architecture modernization, or complex multi-system integrations. For mature products requiring significant technical investment.
Includes:
Architecture redesign and migration planning
Database optimization and scaling
Third-party system integrations
Advanced AI/ML feature development
Performance and security hardening
Team training and handoff
## 4.3 Retainer Services
Ongoing support and iterative development for established partnerships.
