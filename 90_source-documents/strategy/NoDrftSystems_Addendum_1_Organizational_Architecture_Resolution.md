# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This addendum resolves the organizational architecture contradiction identified in the Comprehensive Critical Analysis. Two incompatible organizational models were documented:

Model A (Business Plan): 3-person Centaur pod (Founder/CEO, AI Reliability Engineer, Growth Lead)

Model B (Hiring Framework): 10+ person traditional agency structure with departmental hierarchy

This document formally declares the operative organizational model for Year 1 and establishes the transition path to Year 2+ architecture.

# 1. FORMAL RESOLUTION
RESOLVED: NoDrftSystems shall operate under Model A (Centaur Pod) for Year 1 (Months 1-12). Model B (Full Agency Structure) is designated as the aspirational Year 2-3 target architecture. This resolution is effective immediately and supersedes all conflicting organizational documentation.
## 1.1 Rationale for Resolution
The Centaur Pod model is selected for Year 1 based on the following factors:
Capital Efficiency: $15K seed capital cannot support 10+ FTE salaries
AI-Native Positioning: Centaur model (human + AI) is the core differentiator
Speed to Market: 3-person team can launch in 30 days vs. 90+ days for full agency
Flexibility: Specialist bench provides expertise without fixed overhead
Proof Before Scale: Validate model with revenue before hiring FTEs
