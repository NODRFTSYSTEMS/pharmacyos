# 1. MARKET POSITIONING & DIFFERENTIATION
## 1.1 Unique Value Proposition
NoDrftSystems must establish a clear, defensible position in the market:
Speed-to-Value: Deliver production-ready digital products in 7-30 days vs. industry standard 3-6 months
AI-Native Quality: Every deliverable validated through 5-pass QA with human governance, not just AI generation
Transparent Pricing: Fixed-scope, fixed-price with risk multipliers — no hourly billing surprises
Bilingual Delivery: Native English/Spanish capability for U.S. Latino market and Latin American expansion
Zero Drift Guarantee: Business-context validation, not just functional code
