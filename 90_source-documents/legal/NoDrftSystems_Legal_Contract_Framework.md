# 1. Contract Architecture Overview
## 1.1 Contract Hierarchy
NoDrftSystems uses a three-tier contract structure to streamline engagements and minimize negotiation overhead for repeat clients.
## 1.2 Contract Workflow
Initial client discussion → NDA (if sharing confidential information)
Discovery call → Proposal with SOW outline
Proposal acceptance → MSA + SOW execution
Project kickoff → Work begins upon signed contracts + initial payment

# 2. Master Services Agreement (MSA)
## 2.1 MSA Structure
The MSA establishes the foundational legal relationship between NoDrftSystems and the client.
## 2.2 Key MSA Terms
Term: Initial term of 12 months, auto-renews unless terminated with 30-day notice
Governing Law: Delaware, USA (consistent with LLC formation)
Dispute Resolution: Binding arbitration under AAA rules, Delaware venue
Notice Period: 30 days written notice for termination without cause
Survival Clauses: IP, confidentiality, liability limitations survive termination

# 3. Statement of Work (SOW)
## 3.1 SOW Purpose
Each project requires a separate SOW that references the MSA and defines project-specific terms. The SOW is the binding document for scope, timeline, and payment.
## 3.2 SOW Required Elements
## 3.3 Change Order Process
Scope changes after SOW execution require a formal Change Order:
Client requests change in writing (email acceptable)
NoDrftSystems assesses impact on timeline and budget within 2 business days
Change Order document prepared with revised scope, timeline, and pricing
Both parties sign Change Order before work proceeds
Unapproved changes are out of scope and not performed

# 4. Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
## 4.1 NDA Types
## 4.2 NDA Key Provisions
Definition of Confidential Information: Marked as confidential or reasonably understood to be confidential
Obligations of Receiving Party: Use only for evaluation, protect with reasonable care, limit access
Exceptions: Publicly available, already known, independently developed, required by law
Return of Information: Return or destroy upon request or termination
Remedies: Injunctive relief available for breach

# 5. Intellectual Property Framework
## 5.1 IP Ownership Structure
NoDrftSystems uses a work-for-hire model with specific carve-outs for methodology, tools, and pre-existing IP.
## 5.2 License Grants
NoDrftSystems to Client: Perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to all deliverables
Client to NoDrftSystems: Limited license to use client IP solely for project performance
Portfolio Rights: NoDrftSystems may display work in portfolio unless explicitly restricted
## 5.3 Open Source & Third-Party
NoDrftSystems may use open source and third-party components in deliverables. All such components will be documented with license terms. Client is responsible for compliance with applicable open source licenses in their use of deliverables.

# 6. Data Handling & Privacy
## 6.1 Data Classification
## 6.2 Privacy Compliance
NoDrftSystems adheres to applicable privacy regulations including GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California). Specific commitments:
Data minimization: Only collect data necessary for project performance
Data security: Encryption at rest and in transit, access logging
Data retention: Client data deleted 30 days post-project unless otherwise agreed
Breach notification: Client notified within 24 hours of any suspected data breach
Subprocessor notification: Client notified of any third-party services processing their data
## 6.3 AI & Data Processing
NoDrftSystems uses AI tools that may process client data. Key commitments:
No client data is used to train AI models without explicit written consent
AI tools are selected based on enterprise-grade security and data handling
Client may request list of AI tools used and their data processing terms
Sensitive data (PII, PHI, financial) is anonymized or excluded from AI processing

# 7. Payment Terms & Collections
## 7.1 Standard Payment Structure
## 7.2 Late Payment Policy
Grace Period: 15 days after invoice due date
Late Fee: 1.5% per month (18% APR) on overdue balance
Work Suspension: Work may be suspended after 30 days overdue
Collections: Outstanding balances over 60 days sent to collections
Kill Fee: If client terminates after kickoff, 50% of remaining balance due
## 7.3 Expense Policy
Project quotes include all standard development expenses. Additional expenses require pre-approval and are billed at cost + 15% administrative fee:
Third-party software licenses (if not already owned)
Stock photography, fonts, or design assets
Specialized hosting or infrastructure costs
Travel (if specifically requested by client)

## 8.2 Indemnification

# 9. AI Disclosure Requirements
## 9.1 AI Usage Transparency
NoDrftSystems is an AI-native studio. We disclose our AI usage to clients and comply with emerging AI transparency regulations.
## 9.2 Required Disclosures
All SOWs include the following AI disclosure:
"NoDrftSystems uses artificial intelligence tools in the delivery of services. AI is used for code generation, design assistance, content creation, testing, and quality assurance. All AI-generated outputs are reviewed and validated by human experts before delivery. Client data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws."
## 9.3 Client AI Rights
Opt-Out: Client may request limited AI usage for specific components (may affect timeline/cost)
Transparency: Client may request documentation of AI tools used in their project
Data Protection: Client data is not used to train AI models without explicit consent
