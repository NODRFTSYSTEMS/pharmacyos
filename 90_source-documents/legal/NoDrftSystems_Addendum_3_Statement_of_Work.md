Any changes to the scope, timeline, or pricing of this SOW require a written Change Order signed by both Parties. The Change Order process is:

1. Client submits change request in writing
2. Contractor assesses impact within 2 business days
3. Contractor provides Change Order with revised scope/timeline/pricing
4. Both Parties sign Change Order before work proceeds
