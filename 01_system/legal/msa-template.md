### 1.1 Scope

- **T1 - Conversion Landing Page Sprint:** Single-page conversion-focused website. No CMS.
- **T1S - Static Business Site:** Up to five-page brochure-style website. No CMS.
- **T2 - Business Launch Site:** Up to five-page website with CMS.
- **T3 - Authority Website:** Up to fifteen-page website with advanced CMS, blog, analytics.
- **T4 - Platform Starter:** Web application with defined integrations.
- **T5 - Ecosystem Build:** Multi-surface platform.
