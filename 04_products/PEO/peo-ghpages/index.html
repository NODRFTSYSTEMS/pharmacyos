Methodology

Every number is traceable. Every output is documented.

PEO does not produce estimates you take on faith. The methodology is open, the formulas are published, and every confidence score tells you exactly what it is based on.

Verified ARV

The median sale price of qualified sold comparables — not an AVM. Actual closed transactions within a defined radius and recency window.

Multi-Gate Confidence

Each analysis passes through data quality, comp coverage, valuation consistency, and model completeness gates before a confidence tier is assigned.

Transparent Limitations

PEO is not a licensed appraisal. Rural markets and off-market properties may have insufficient comp coverage. All limitations are disclosed at the output level.

Common Questions

Answers before you commit.

What is Peak Equity Optimizer?

PEO is a real-estate intelligence platform that helps sellers understand their net proceeds and helps investors analyze deals with verified ARV, live comps, and strategy-specific underwriting.

Where does the data come from?

Paid tiers pull live county assessor records and MLS-recorded sold comparables at the time of analysis. Free tiers are manual-input only.

What is the difference between Investor Basic and Investor Advanced?

Basic includes automatic ARV, comps, MAO, profit, ROI, and a summary report. Advanced adds comp controls, line-item rehab budgeting, scenario planning, DSCR, refi modeling, and advanced appendices.

Is the Seller tier a subscription?

No. The Seller tier is a one-time payment per property analysis ($49). You pay when you run an analysis — not on a recurring basis.
