1. About CasaClaro

CasaClaro is a real estate marketplace operated by NoDrft Systems, connecting international buyers with Colombian real estate. The platform serves users in Medellín, Bogotá, Cartagena, and other Colombian cities.

2. Commission Structure

CasaClaro charges a 3% commission on completed property transactions facilitated through the platform. This fee is:

  • Charged to the seller upon successful closing of a sale
  • Calculated on the final agreed sale price in USD or COP equivalent
  • Not charged for listing submission or property marketing
  • Due within 30 days of the closing date

No listing fees are charged. Listings remain active for 90 days and may be renewed at no cost.

3. Property Listings

By submitting a property listing, you represent that:

  • You are the owner of record or have legal authority to list the property
  • All information provided is accurate and not misleading
  • You hold clear title or are authorized to sell
  • Photos submitted are owned by you or you hold rights to use them

CasaClaro reserves the right to remove listings that violate these terms or applicable Colombian law.

4. Data Privacy — Colombian Law 1581 of 2012

CasaClaro collects personal data including name, email address, phone number, financial information, and uploaded photographs for the purpose of facilitating real estate transactions. In accordance with Colombian Law 1581 of 2012 (Habeas Data), you have the right to:

  • Know, update, and rectify your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Revoke consent for data processing
  • Access your personal data free of charge

To exercise any of these rights, contact: privacy@casaclaro.co

Your data will not be sold to third parties. Data may be shared with notaries, legal representatives, and financial institutions solely as required to complete a transaction.
