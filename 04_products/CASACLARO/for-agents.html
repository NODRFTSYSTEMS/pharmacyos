Response expectations are explicit

How Routing Works

CasaClaro sends context, not chaos

  • Investor leads carry city, budget, timeline, and stated goal.
  • Seller leads carry pricing, condition, repair notes, and attachment metadata.
  • Relocation-driven clients arrive with healthcare, walkability, and retiree-fit concerns already surfaced.
  • Partners are expected to respond clearly and update status instead of leaving the system blind.

Marketplace Discipline

What strong partners do differently

  • Translate neighborhood fit into plain language for foreigners and casual market entrants.
  • Explain repair, legal, and HOA friction early instead of burying it.
  • Respect response-time targets because high-intent international clients interpret silence as risk.
  • Work well with lawyers, managers, and contractors instead of acting like the sale is the whole system.

Who fits

  • Agents and brokers who can explain pricing, regulations, and neighborhood fit clearly.
  • Lawyers who understand title, foreign investment registration, and contract review.
  • Property managers who can stabilize long-stay or hospitality assets after closing.
  • Contractors who can inspect, quote, and communicate repairs without vague optimism.
