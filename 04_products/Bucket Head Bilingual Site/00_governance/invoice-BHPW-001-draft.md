### Decision 1 — Tier Confirmation (REQUIRED)

**Issue:** The T1S tier covers up to 5 pages at $3,500. This build delivered 6 pages.

**Options:**

| Option | Billing | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| A | T1S ($3,500) — treat 6th page as included within goodwill scope | Simplest; defensible for a small local business client |
| B | T1S ($3,500) + hourly add-on for 6th page at $150/hr | Requires Change Order; estimate ~3–4 hrs = $450–600 |
| C | T2 base ($5,500) — next tier up; accounts for full scope | T2 includes CMS which was not built; over-prices the delivery; not recommended unless Founder determines T2 is the right anchor |

**Recommendation:** Option A — T1S at $3,500 base. The 6th page is marginal and within goodwill scope for a first local business client. Log the decision.
