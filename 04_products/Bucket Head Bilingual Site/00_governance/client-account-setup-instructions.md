## What You Need to Do

You have four tasks. Complete them in order and send us the information listed at the end of each section.

## Task 1 — Buy Your Domain Name

Your domain name is your web address. You need to purchase `bucketheadmobile.com`.

**Where to buy it:**

We recommend **Namecheap** — it is affordable and easy to use.

1. Go to **namecheap.com**
2. Search for `bucketheadmobile.com`
3. Add it to your cart and check out
4. Create an account using **edwardjohnsoncompanybuckethead@gmail.com**
5. Complete the purchase (expect to pay approximately $10–12 per year)

> **Important:** Use `edwardjohnsoncompanybuckethead@gmail.com` for all accounts in this guide — it keeps everything connected to one inbox and is already displayed on your website.

**When you are done, send us:**
- Confirmation that you purchased `bucketheadmobile.com`
- The email address you used to create your Namecheap account

We will send you DNS instructions (a short list of settings to enter in Namecheap) after you confirm the purchase.

---

## Task 2 — Create a GitHub Account

GitHub is where your website's files are stored. You need a free account so we can transfer ownership of your website to you.

1. Go to **github.com**
2. Click **Sign up**
3. Click **Continue with Google** and select your `edwardjohnsoncompanybuckethead@gmail.com` account
4. Choose a username — we suggest something like `bucketheadpw` or your business name
5. Select the **Free** plan

**When you are done, send us:**
- Your GitHub username

We will transfer the website files to your account.

---

## Task 3 — Create a Formspree Account (Contact Form)

Formspree handles the messages customers send through your Contact page. Without this, form submissions will not reach you.

1. Go to **formspree.io**
2. Click **Get Started**
3. Click **Continue with Google** and select your `edwardjohnsoncompanybuckethead@gmail.com` account
4. Once inside, click **+ New Form**
5. Name the form: `Bucket Head Contact`
6. Copy the **Form ID** — it looks like 8 random letters and numbers (example: `xkgbwnqp`)

**When you are done, send us:**
- Your Formspree Form ID (the 8-character code)

We will wire this into your website so contact form submissions go directly to your email.

---

## Task 4 — Get Your Google Review Link

Your website has a section that invites customers to leave you a Google review. We need your unique Google Business review link to make that button work.

**How to find your link:**

1. Go to **business.google.com** and log into your Google Business Profile
2. Click on your business listing
3. On the left menu, click **Get more reviews** (or search for "review link" in the dashboard)
4. Copy the short link Google provides — it looks like: `g.page/r/...`

If you do not have a Google Business Profile yet, go to **business.google.com** and create one. It is free and helps customers find you on Google Maps.

**When you are done, send us:**
- Your Google review short link
