## Status Definitions

- `Draft`: Working version, not yet reviewed
- `In QA`: Under QA review, blockers possible
- `QA Hold`: Blocked — see QA report
- `QA Pass`: QA cleared, awaiting human approval
- `Approved`: Human-approved, client-ready
- `Delivered`: Transferred to client
