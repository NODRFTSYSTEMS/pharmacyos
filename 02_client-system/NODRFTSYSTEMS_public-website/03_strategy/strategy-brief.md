# Strategy Brief - NoDrftSystems Website Governance Reconciliation

## Objective

Convert the NoDrftSystems website from a structurally active but partially ungoverned product surface into a fully paired, reviewable, public commercial route system with controlled proof posture, explicit release blockers, and clean control-layer references.

## Verified Facts

- `04_products/nOdRFTsYS WebSite/` exists as an active product folder.
- `04_products/nOdRFTsYS WebSite/REVIEW-SUMMARY.md` records the current website release status as `HOLD`.
- The review summary identifies four active blockers:
  - bilingual human editorial review of `#/es/` routing behavior
  - legal review of the expanded Terms of Service
  - live secure form endpoints must be populated at deploy time
  - final integrated Lighthouse and manual QA pass still pending
- The 2026-04-19 business plan identified the absence of a paired `02_client-system/` workspace for this website as a governance gap.

## Scope

- create the paired proprietary workspace and control artifacts for the website
- reconcile control-plane and document-registry records
- activate company-baseline closure artifacts that govern proof, pricing unknowns, and website status
- track website release blockers in a governed execution plan

## Exclusions

- no invention of legal entity facts, live revenue metrics, or live financial baselines
- no approval of public proof claims without a populated proof inventory
- no operative routine-usage pricing figures
- no public release while review-summary blockers remain open

## Delivery Direction

1. Close the structural governance gap first.
2. Keep the site in `HOLD` until the release blockers are actually resolved.
3. Treat trust surfaces as product features:
   - proof posture
   - CTA routing
   - bilingual parity
   - legal page integrity
   - secure endpoint configuration
4. Use the company-baseline protocol so unresolved business facts stay bounded instead of bleeding into the site.

## Acceptance Criteria

- paired workspace exists and contains the minimum proprietary product control artifacts
- repository control plane explicitly pairs the website product folder with this workspace
- document registry records both the workspace and the active product documents
- business plan no longer describes the website governance state as unresolved
- website remains on `HOLD` until the four review-summary blockers are closed through governed review
